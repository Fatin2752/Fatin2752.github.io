My Education

I began my educational journey at age five years old. Then, I move to the Batu Laut primary school when I was seven years old until twelve years old. After finish studied at the primary school, I continued my studied at the Unwanus Saadah high school until I got a great result in (SPM). Later, I continued my studies at Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM) in Kedah, where I take a course in Diploma of Informatic Library and I met some of the most inspiring friends and lecturer who helped me grow in academically.
